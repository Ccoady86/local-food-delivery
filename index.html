Our Services

Local Food Catering

We provide the best catering service in the whole of Waterford city and counnty. With a range of fresh delicious meals created to suit whatever your event may be, we can accomodate functions large or small. Our expert chefs will prepare your food either on-site or in our kitchens. Our friendly catering staff will be on hand for the duration of your function to serve your guests while providing a five star professional service. Best of all, we will clean up afterwards so all you have to do is enjoy the company of your guests.

Having a Party?

Why not try our party delivery option? Take the hassle out of food preparation and let us take care of everything! Just tell us how many guests to allow for and we will deliver a host of pre-made party food options straight to your door. From nachos to pizza, chicken goujons to potatoe wedges, even luxurious beef wellington, all you have to do is pop them in the microwave or re-heat in the oven and give your guests a night to remember.

Home Dinner Delivery

Sometimes after a hard day, the last thing you want to do is cook! That is why we offer a full take away menu that can be delivered piping hot straight to your door. Order one of our locally prepared home dinners and enjoy a hearty feast such as bacon and cabbage, Sunday roast chicken or succulent rack of lamb and enjoy your hard earned time off!
